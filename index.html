The Sacred Synopsis

Torn between ancient vows and dark desires...

Aurelia Silver-Breast is no ordinary angel. As the last of the Winged Wet-Nurses, her celestial nectar is the only source of pure magic capable of keeping the infant star-gods alive. Bound by the absolute, chaste Vow of the Milky Veil, she must never let a mortal or shadow-being touch her skin. She is the light of the heavens—untouched, untouchable, and utterly alone.

"His feathers were midnight, but his touch was pure white fire."

Enter Malakor Void-Wing, the brooding Prince of the Shadow Realm. His obsidian wings are scarred, his heart is locked in ice, and his kingdom is dying of the creeping dust-rot. He needs the holy power of Aurelia's nectar to save his people. When he kidnaps her to his crumbling gothic cathedral, he expects to take her magic by force. What he doesn't expect is a fierce, defiant angel who stirs a raw, unquenchable hunger within his dark soul.

As the dark stars align and the Sun Spires crumble, Aurelia's silver wings flare with forbidden heat. She must choose: uphold her sacred duty and let the Shadow Realm perish, or surrender to the obsidian Prince and feed the darkness that threatens to consume them both.

The Winged Cohort

Meet the players in this celestial ballet of passion

Aurelia Silver-Breast

The Winged Wet-Nurse

Gentle yet fiercely protective, Aurelia has spent her life in the Sun Spires nursing the cosmic infants. She believes her destiny is pure service, but Malakor's rough, dark touch awakens longings she never knew existed.

"My nectar is for the star-gods, Malakor. It does not belong to a monster like you."

Malakor Void-Wing

The Brooding Prince of Dust

Scarred by wars against the Sky Realm, Malakor rules his dying land with ruthless precision. Armed with dark void magic and massive black wings, he is prepared to destroy the heavens—until he holds the Winged Wet-Nurse in his arms.

"Your vows are meaningless in my kingdom, angel. Feed me, or watch your sky burn."

Seraphina Gold-Feather

The Jealous High Priestess

The high-ranking guardian of the Sun Spires. Seraphina has secret ambitions to sit on the Solar Throne. Devious and golden-winged, she suspects Aurelia's hidden desires and plots to have her wings clipped for heresy.

"She is tainted, Malakor! Her milk is poisoned with your shadow. She deserves the clipper's blade!"
